Chauffagistes – Information

Mining modes, how rewards work, and the project vision. Latency is optimised for France thanks to our redundant hosting in Île-de-France.

Infrastructure

The pool runs on a node hosted by us in Île-de-France, with redundancy on another local site.
Advantage: low latency for miners located in France.

Available mining modes
Pure solo mining
Who is it for

For those who want to mine alone without maintaining their own node. If you already run a node, this mode won’t bring additional benefits.

Sovereignty tip: running a local node is quick, inexpensive, and gives strong advantages. We recommend it.
Connection

stratum+tcp://chauffagistes-pool.fr:3333
AxeOS: URL: chauffagistes-pool.fr – Port: 3333
User: [Your on-chain receiving address] — Password: x

Rewards

The block reward goes directly to your wallet, with no fees or intermediaries.

Advantages
  • 100% of the reward
  • No node maintenance
Private collaborative mining
Who is it for

For friends/communities who want to mine together privately and share rewards fairly without exposing a public node.

Connection

stratum+tcp://chauffagistes-pool.fr:3333
User: [Your on-chain address].[userID] — Password: x
Example: bc1qkt...ae.Satoshi
(These identifiers are public if a block is found.)

Rewards & optional split

The reward is paid to the wallet defined as User.
Optional split formula: [Sum of your share difficulties] / [Sum of your group’s difficulties] → gives each miner’s effective contribution over the period.

Advantages
  • 100% of the reward, split as you wish
  • No node management
  • Transparent calculation of effective work
Pool mining
Who is it for

For those who want to maximise the odds of finding a block by pooling their hashrate.

Connection

stratum+tcp://chauffagistes-pool.fr:3333
User: [Pool’s multi-sig address].[yourID] — Password: x
(Identifiers are visible if a block is found.)

Rewards & security

Rewards go to a multi-signature wallet held by several members. No one can act alone. Real-time split is computed as:
[Sum of a user’s share difficulties] / [Total group difficulties]

Advantages
  • Much higher chance of finding a block
  • Fair split based on effective work (PoW quality)
  • Helps smaller miners stay motivated
About

Born from a group exploring Bitcoin more seriously since 2024, with the idea of heating while mining and ensuring maximum transparency in reward distribution. We build upon the open-source ckpool base and opened our work to the community since summer 2025.

We take no fees: donations and our own miners support the project. We believe small home mining devices will have a place in many households — which requires simple, fair and transparent tools.
