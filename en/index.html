Solo mining

  1. Configure your miner (or stratum proxy):
    Stratum URL:
    stratum+tcp://chauffagistes-pool.fr:3333
    Credentials (user/pass):
    btc_address.worker x
  2. Check your address on the Statistics page (search user).
  3. Need help? Join our Discord.

Mining & Heating: a synergy

The name “Chauffagistes” is no coincidence: by reusing the heat generated from Bitcoin mining, we can heat homes or collective spaces while securing the network. Several members are already testing integrations in France.
